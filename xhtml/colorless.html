Colorless Tsukuru Tazaki and His Years of Pilgrimage

Release Date: 2013 (Original Japanese Edition)

Genre: Contemporary Fiction, Mystery

Description: "Colorless Tsukuru Tazaki and His Years of Pilgrimage" is a compelling novel by the celebrated Japanese author Haruki Murakami. The story follows Tsukuru Tazaki, a young man haunted by the sudden rejection of his closest friends during his college years. Struggling to understand the reasons behind their abandonment, Tsukuru embarks on a journey of self-discovery and reconciliation. As he delves into his past and seeks out his former friends, he uncovers long-buried secrets and confronts the lingering shadows of his youth. Set against the backdrop of modern-day Japan, the narrative explores themes of friendship, identity, and the search for belonging. Murakami's evocative prose and masterful storytelling create a poignant and introspective exploration of the human condition, inviting readers to contemplate the complexities of memory, loss, and the passage of time.

Main Characters:

  • Tsukuru Tazaki (Male)
  • Sara Kimoto (Female)
  • Ao Satsuki (Female)

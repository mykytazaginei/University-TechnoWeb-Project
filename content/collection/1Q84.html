1Q84

Release Date: 2009-2010 (Original Japanese Edition)

Genre: Speculative Fiction, Magical Realism

Description: "1Q84" is a mesmerizing and expansive novel by the acclaimed Japanese author Haruki Murakami. Set in Tokyo during the year 1984, the narrative unfolds across three intertwining storylines, where the lives of two protagonists, Aomame and Tengo, become enmeshed in a world that defies conventional reality. Aomame, a fitness instructor and assassin, finds herself drawn into a mysterious organization known as the Dowager, while Tengo, an aspiring writer and math tutor, becomes involved in a literary conspiracy surrounding a teenage girl's manuscript. As the boundary between the familiar and the surreal blurs, Aomame and Tengo navigate a landscape where alternate realities coexist, marked by the eerie presence of two moons in the sky. Murakami's evocative prose and masterful storytelling transport readers into a realm where questions of identity, love, and destiny converge in unexpected ways.

Main Characters:

  • Aomame (Female)
  • Tengo (Male)
  • Fuka-Eri (Female)
